    I believe that the first questions suggest a wrong answer as good, please correct me if I am wrong:

    – 40 visitors with a 4.80% Conversion rate means that you have 1.92 conversions.
    – So the SE (Standard error) is 3.38%

    – The Confidence Interval for a 90% significance level is: p+-(1.65 x SE):
    so for this ad is: 0% – 10.38%

    So the conversion rate of this ad in any sample period would be, 9 out of 10 times, between 0% and 10.38%

    That means:
    – The sample is too small and the SE too big to draw any valid conclusion yet
    – The 4.8% CR ad IS NOT better than the other with a significance level of 90%

    Therefore the correct answer is that the sample is too small.
